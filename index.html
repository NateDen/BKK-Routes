BTS Route Planner

How to Use

Click the starting location and ending location on the map. Press go to get the price (an estimate), and reset to start over. Press the switch at slider if you have further transportation, and enter your prices (if you don't know, 16 for buses and 20 for motorcycles is a good starting point). Press total to get the total price of the route.
